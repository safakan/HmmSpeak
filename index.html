HmmSpeak

Breaking language barriers through natural conversation practice

Why We Built This

HmmSpeak was built to help language learners practice speaking in the most natural way possible - through real conversations with friends or language partners.

How to Use

Simply start a practice session and begin talking with your conversation partner. When you get stuck or need inspiration, glance at the screen for helpful suggestions, vocabulary, and conversation prompts.

Our Goal

Our goal is to make language practice feel effortless and enjoyable, removing the pressure of formal exercises while providing gentle guidance when needed.
